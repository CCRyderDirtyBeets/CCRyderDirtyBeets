The Music

CC Ryder & Dirty Beets is a Boulder Based Band, Creating Unique Covers, and Original Music that can adapt to all of your musical needs. CC Ryder & Dirty Beets have mixed diverse styles of Rock, Rap, Reggae, Folk, Funk, Americana and Bluegrass, into a unique sound with a lively landscape and energy.


Meet The Band

CC Ryder & Dirty Beets is comprised of Chris Cullen (Guitar, Lead Vocals) Joel McKee (Bass, Vocals) Mike McDonnell (Guitar/Vocals) Nate Wilkinson (Keys/Vocals)Adam Hines (Elec. Banjo) and Brennan Gildersleeve (Drums/Dirty Beets).
